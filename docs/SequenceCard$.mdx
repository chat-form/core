## SequenceCard

### When to use

SequenceCard is suitable for displaying a series of steps, usually a directed acyclic graph (DAG). For example [this graph](https://lf3-static.bytednsdoc.com/obj/eden-cn/fvyaeh7pfuhpaps/dag.png):

When navigate back to a previous step, all the steps after it will be reset, since the active route may change.
